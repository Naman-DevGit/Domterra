Simple, local property listings you can trust

Domterra helps owners and buyers connect directly — from city flats to farmland — with clear listings and no hidden fees.

Who we are

Domterra is a student-built real-estate project focused on building a simple, transparent marketplace for property owners and buyers in Satna and nearby areas. We emphasise verified listings, straightforward contact between owner and buyer, and a curated selection of niche properties such as farmland and weekend homes.

How it works

  • Owners add free listings with photos and details.
  • Buyers browse and contact owners directly via listing contact info.
  • We spot-check listings to keep quality high.
